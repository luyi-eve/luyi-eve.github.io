Hi! My name is Yi Lu and my friends call me ✨ Eve ✨

- Tools I use: Python (Pandas, BeautifulSoup, Selenium), HTML/CSS, JavaScript/D3.js, Adobe Illustrator, Mapbox, QGIS, Datawrapper/Flourish, etc.


- Recently, I just graduated from Stanford University with a Master's degree in Data Journalism for the 2023-24 academic year and I am currently interning for the Tampa Bay Times as a data reporter.

- Prior to Stanford, I hold an M.A. degree in Journalism at the University of Miami and I am also a recent alumna of the Lede Program 2022 at Columbia Journalism School. In 2020, I graduated from Shanghai University with a bachelor's degree in Journalism.


- I grew up in the Chinese city of Shanghai and speak English and Mandarin. I worked for Bytedance as an editor and was a TV show director assistant at The International Channel Shanghai. I also wrote for Chinese newspapers.


I'm also an amateur cartoonist🎨 with years of practice in sketching who only draws on diary books. Scroll down to the bottom if you'd like to check my original digital illustration and sketch art reproduction:)
